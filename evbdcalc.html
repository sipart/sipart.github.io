Important disclaimer
This calculator estimates EV battery degradation using public battery-health findings and simplified assumptions. It is not a diagnostic tool and cannot replace manufacturer battery checks, warranty assessments, BMS data or OBD readings.
Sources and assumptions
The model uses a broad public average degradation rate and published observations about increased stress from high-power DC charging, hot climates, high/low state-of-charge dwell time, temperature, operating window and cycling patterns. Vehicle specifications in the built-in list are typical approximate values and should be verified before publication.
